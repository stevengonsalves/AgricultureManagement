Agriculture Management System (AMS)
The Agriculture Management System (AMS) connects farmers and buyers, ensuring quality and transparency through technology. This system revolutionizes transactions and offers potential for broader reach and sustainability in the agriculture sector.
Introduction
The Agriculture Management System (AMS) is designed to bridge the gap between farmers and buyers by providing a platform for seamless transactions. AMS aims to promote transparency, ensure quality, and expand market reach, contributing to sustainable agricultural practices.
Features
User Registration: Farmers and buyers can create and manage their accounts.
Product Listing: Farmers can list their products with detailed descriptions and pricing.
Order Management: Buyers can place orders, and farmers can manage orders efficiently.
Quality Assurance: Mechanisms to ensure product quality and transparency in transactions.
Notifications: Real-time notifications for order updates and other important information.
